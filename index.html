EDUC8510 - Yilin Gong, Stella Aideyan, Faith Ezekwe

About the Course

This course explores how Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) can support teaching and learning within an educational context. It analyzes principles, strategies, and related issues regarding the design of innovative educational technologies and creative learning environments.

The course will include discussions on readings and hands-on learning activities. Participants will gain an understanding of how various Information and Communication Technologies (ICT) enhance a student-centered approach to higher-order learning, enabling them to implement strategies that engage students and foster critical thinking.
